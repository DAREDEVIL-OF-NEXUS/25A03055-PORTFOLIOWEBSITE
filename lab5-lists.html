Ordered Recipe — Making Tea

  1. Boil 1 cup of water.
  2. Add tea leaves (or tea bags).
  3. Optional: Add ginger/cardamom.
  4. Simmer 1–2 minutes.
  5. Add milk; bring to gentle boil.
  6. Add sugar to taste; strain and serve.
